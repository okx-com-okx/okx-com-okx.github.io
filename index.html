Security Measures
Security is paramount in the cryptocurrency space, and OKX takes this aspect seriously. The platform employs multiple layers of security protocols to protect users’ assets and personal information.

Cold Wallet Storage: The majority of user funds are stored in cold wallets, which are offline and less susceptible to hacking attempts. This ensures that most of the cryptocurrency is kept safe, with only a small portion held in hot wallets for liquidity purposes.

Two-Factor Authentication (2FA): OKX requires users to enable 2FA, adding an extra layer of protection to user accounts. This additional step helps prevent unauthorized access, enhancing overall account security.

Regular Security Audits: The exchange undergoes regular security audits and employs a dedicated security team to monitor for potential vulnerabilities. This proactive approach helps to identify and address any security concerns promptly.

Additionally, OKX occasionally offers promotional trading fee discounts, further incentivizing users to trade on the platform. However, it’s essential for users to review the fee schedule regularly, as fees may vary depending on market conditions and promotions.
